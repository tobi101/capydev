# Telegram Mini App in 14 Days

## Client Goal
Launch a Telegram mini app for merch sales and pre-orders on collectible figurines.

## Project Team
- 1 tech lead / architect
- 2 frontend developers (TypeScript + TMA SDK)
- 1 UX designer
- 1 QA engineer

## Phases
1. **Briefing (1 day)** — co-created the user flow and guardrails.
2. **Prototyping (3 days)** — interactive Figma prototype plus quick user testing.
3. **Development (7 days)** — integrated the client's back office, payments via Stars and bank cards.
4. **Testing & Launch (3 days)** — automated E2E tests and device runs on real users.

## Key Results
- Average order value: **3,200 ₽**
- Conversion from Telegram broadcast to purchase: **11.5%**
- Support for three languages and dark/light themes

## Highlights
- Mini-game with quick battles for rare prizes (+23% retention)
- Live delivery status notifications
- Deferred payments and webhooks for automated logistics
